Cross-platform App Development

Particle41 partnered with Superchat to build their app from the ground up.

Superchat is a social media app unlike anything out there! It is for everyone who loves hanging out with friends while watching videos, but wishes they could do so even when they are not together.

Superchat enables you to chat with your friends who are on other messaging platforms, all without having to leave Superchat. No more jumping from messaging app to messaging app to chat with your friends.
